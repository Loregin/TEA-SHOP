ABOUT THE TEA

MILKTEA LOVERS

Simply put, milk tea is tea with milk added. This mixture gives typically bitter teas, like black tea, a smoothness and slight sweetness. Anything can be made from milk tea, including masala chai and bubble tea.

History Of Milktea

Black or green tea, milk, and tapioca pearls are combined to make boba tea, also referred to as pearl milk tea or bubble tea. As they are meant to be sucked on like bubbles, the balls or pearls are what give the beverage its name. Back in the 1980s, Taichung City in Taiwan was the birthplace of this sweet and energizing drink.
